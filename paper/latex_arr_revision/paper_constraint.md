# Paper Revision Constraints

When modifying this LaTeX revision, always check the following constraints before finalizing changes:

1. The paper must not exceed 8 pages before the Limitation section. The Limitation section and appendices are excluded from this page limit.
2. Figure 1 must be placed at the top of the right column on the first page.
3. Use the term "frameworks" consistently. Do not use "scaffold" or "scaffolds" when referring to agent frameworks.
4. Do not use "SKU" or "SKUs" in manuscript prose. Use "product" or "products" instead.
